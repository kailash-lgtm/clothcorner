Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information Anokhi by Cloth Corner collects when you use our website or contact us on WhatsApp, and how we use it.

Last updated: 16 July 2026

1. Who We Are

Anokhi by Cloth Corner is a boutique clothing store at 5th Floor, Alankar Place, Boring Rd Crossing, Anandpuri, Patna, Bihar 800001, India. This policy applies to the anokhiclothcorner.com website and to our WhatsApp Business number, +91 87973 34740.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you choose to give us, including:

  • Contact form: your name, 10-digit phone number, and the message you type (for example, the product, size or colour you are asking about). Submitting the form opens WhatsApp with this information pre-filled so you can send it to us directly — we do not store form submissions on a server.
  • WhatsApp messages: when you message us on WhatsApp (via the "WhatsApp Order" button, floating WhatsApp icon, or by messaging +91 87973 34740 directly), we receive your WhatsApp phone number, profile name, and the content of your messages, so we can respond to your enquiry or order.
  • Phone / email: if you call or email us directly using the contact details on this site.

We do not use cookies, analytics trackers, or advertising pixels on this website. The site loads Google Fonts to display our typography, which may cause your browser to request font files from Google's servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your enquiries about our products (sarees, suits, salwars, nighties, blouses, petticoats).
  • To discuss and confirm orders, pricing, sizing and availability.
  • To share order or store updates with you, if you have contacted us.
  • To improve our products and customer service based on feedback you share.

We do not use your information for automated marketing messages without your consent, and we do not sell or rent your personal information to any third party.

4. WhatsApp & Meta

When you message us on WhatsApp, your conversation is also subject to WhatsApp's own Privacy Policy, since WhatsApp is operated by WhatsApp LLC / Meta Platforms, Inc. We use WhatsApp only as a messaging channel to communicate with customers who choose to contact us there — we do not have any independent access to your WhatsApp account beyond the chat you have with our business number.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Where required to fulfil your order (for example, a courier/delivery partner, if you request home delivery).
  • Where required by law, court order, or government request.

6. Data Retention

We keep WhatsApp conversations and contact details for as long as reasonably necessary to handle your enquiry or order, maintain our business records, and meet any legal requirements, after which they may be deleted.

7. Your Rights

You can ask us, at any time, to tell you what information we hold about you, to correct it, or to delete it (for example, by deleting our WhatsApp conversation on your device and asking us to delete it on ours). Contact us using the details below to make such a request.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website and WhatsApp ordering are intended for use by adults placing enquiries or orders. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page shows when it was last revised.
